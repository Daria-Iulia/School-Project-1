Introduction


Purpose

The purpose of this website is to keep people up to date with the latest news and announcements regarding The Birmingham 2022 Commonwealth Games, the participants and their stories.


Target Audience

The target audience would be anyone that's intrested in olympic sports and want to quicky check for the news regarding the events and the athletes that attend it.


Design summary

A simple news scroll bar will allow users to surf through the interface in a few clicks and find all the information they need. If the article is clicked, it expands on the centre of the screen.
